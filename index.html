About Us

Saransh Sewa Trust is a dedicated non-profit organization in Kamapur, UP. We aim to uplift underprivileged individuals and communities through compassion, education, and essential support during times of need.

What We Do

  • 📚 Education for children and adults
  • 🍽️ Distribution of food and essentials
  • 🤝 Crisis support for families in need
  • 🌱 Community empowerment initiatives
